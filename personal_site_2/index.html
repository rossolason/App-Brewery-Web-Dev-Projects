Renewable Energy

SpaceX - Starlink

From 2019-2022, I was a solar array engineer working on SpaceX's Starlink satellites. In this role, I was responsible for the operation and health monitoring of more space solar arrays than any other human in history!

SpaceX - Crew Dragon

From 2013-2019, I was a structures engineer working on the crew Dragon capsule. I designed most of the structural components in the capsule service section, including the structures repsonsible for reacting the thrust of all SuperDraco abort motors.

Education and FSAE

From 2009-2013, I studied mechanical engineering at the University of Washington. I was the drivetrain lead and one of the drivers for the UW FSAE racecar team. In my senior year, we won one of the two US competitions. We also built the first electric car in team history.
